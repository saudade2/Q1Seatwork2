The Characters in Noli me Tangere

Image Name of the Character Description of the Character
Chrisostomo Ibarra Chrisostomo Ibarra is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra, which was the richest person in San Diego, he is also the main character of the story. In the beginning of the story Chrisostomo Ibarra just arrived at the party held by Kapitan Tiago after 7 years of studying in Europe. Chrisostomo Ibarra's goals was to establish a school in the Philippines following his stay in Europe.
Don Rafael Ibarra Don Rafael is the father of the Main character, Chrisostomo Ibarra. He was the richest person in San Diego, but was unjustly imprisoned and killed by Padre Damaso and others, on charges of murder of an official, he was branded as a heretic and a filibuster. Don Rafael was described as being generous and having good morals.
Maria Clara Maria Clara is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago(real father is Padre Damaso), and also the fiancee of Chrisostomo Ibarra under an arranged marriage by Don Rafael and Kapitan Tiago. She is described as being very beautiful and kind, with everyone around liking her.
Padre Damaso He is the antagonist of the story, and also the real father of Maria Clara. Padre Damaso is a former curate of San Diego,a priest, and a friar. He wrongly accused Don Rafael on the murder of a tax official, and also strongly opposed the relationship and later broke off the marriage between Chrisostomo Ibarra and Maria Clara. He is described as a greedy, arrogant, and mean character.
Saturnino Saturnino is the self-proclaimed son of the old spaniard who bought the forest near San Diego, but the old spaniard later died by hanging on a branch on a tree. After Saturnino established himself in San Diego, he realized he is becoming old, so he married a woman from Manila, which gave birth to a child, the child was named Don Rafael. Saturnino is described as a hardworking individual, but also has a violent and impetuous(?) attitude.
